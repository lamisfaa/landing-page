## 2. Problem Section

Use this section to describe the learner’s pain points.

### Problems Implied By The Current Website

- Students often do not know their real English level before choosing a course.
- Many learners need a clear path instead of random lessons or disconnected tutoring.
- Learners want a teacher who understands Arabic and can explain in a culturally familiar way.
- Students need flexible scheduling because they are balancing school, work, family, or travel.
- IELTS and STEP candidates need focused exam preparation, not general English content.
- Students worry whether online lessons will actually be interactive and personalized.
- Learners want proof of progress, reports, certificates, and a clear next level.
- Some students are hesitant to pay before understanding the curriculum or teacher.

### Problem Copy Options

Many English learners start with the wrong level, switch between random lessons, or study without knowing what they should improve next.

Most online platforms give you access to tutors, but not always a clear path, a fixed teacher, progress reports, or bilingual support.

For IELTS and STEP, general practice is not enough. Students need focused preparation based on real exam patterns, correction, and clear strategy.

## 3. Solution Section

### Main Solution

Tutorings gives each student a structured learning journey: first identify the student’s level, choose the right program, schedule lessons at a suitable time, then start learning with a private teacher.

1. Identify your English level.

3. Schedule the program at a time that works for you.

- Students can take a free placement test before registration.

- The teacher stays fixed during the level.

- Programs are structured around recognized levels such as CEFR.

## 9. FAQ Section

Use corrected versions because the current website appears to have some mismatched FAQ answers.

### FAQ Items

Are there male and female teachers?
Yes. Tutorings has professional male and female teachers from different backgrounds, and they speak English and Arabic fluently to make learning easier.

Can I pause the program if I travel or become busy?
Yes. Students can request to freeze the program for up to 14 days, as long as they notify the team in advance.

Can I watch the live lecture later?
Yes. A recorded version becomes available in the application after the live lecture for review.

Will I study with the same teacher throughout the program?
Yes. Tutorings assigns a fixed teacher during the level to follow the student’s progress and understand their weaknesses.

Why is the program private?
Because all lessons are one-to-one and customized according to the student’s goals, weaknesses, and learning needs.

How much time should I study daily?
It depends on the student’s level and goals, but the website recommends around 30-40 minutes daily outside lesson time for review and homework. IELTS preparation mentions a higher study commitment of around 1.5 to 2 hours daily.

Will I receive a certificate?
Yes. Students receive a certificate after completing the level and passing the advancement test.
